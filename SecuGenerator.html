This is just a basic security gate for remoteqth hardware. Its not strong enough to avoid heavy attacs and the sourcecode is available on our webside. But it has 16bit strong password protection and some basic algorythm not that easy to guess... And its an arduino... Your hardware is pretty much protected from normal hackers or script kiddies. So dont worry! :)

Key: (must be exact 16 characters dont use [ or ] or ' or " or / or \ or { or } or =)
Salt: (try a 2-5 digit number not ending 2,5 or 0)



1. Copy the choosen Salt value to the variable called "salt" in the mega_webswitch.js file on your webserver/local storage!

2. Copy the choosen Salt value to the variable called "intSalt" in the config.cfg file on your sd card!

AND

3. Copy the choosen Key value to the variable called "strToken" in the config.cfg file on your sd card!
